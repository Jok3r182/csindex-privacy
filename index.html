Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 9, 2025

Overview

CSIndex ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

We keep it simple: we do not collect, store, or share your personal data.

Information We Do Not Collect

CSIndex does not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Account credentials or login data
  • Location data
  • Photos or camera images (scans are processed on-device only)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers

Camera Usage

CSIndex requests access to your camera solely to scan CS2 inspect screens. Images captured by the camera are processed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework. No images are uploaded to any server.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your saved skins are stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can delete it at any time by removing the app.

Third-Party Services

CSIndex fetches skin pricing data from the CSFloat marketplace API. Requests to CSFloat include skin name and float parameters only — no personal information is sent. Please refer to CSFloat's Privacy Policy for their data practices.

In-app purchases are handled by Apple's StoreKit. We do not process or store any payment information.

Children's Privacy

CSIndex is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
